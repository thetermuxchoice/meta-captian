If you need a new website

New project Consultancy

Do you need a new website for your project, but you don’t know where to start?

We will assess your project, objectives, and available budget. We offer you an external and independent opinion on the viability of your project, technology and trends, so it can be developed at the best possible value for money.

We team up with your company’s marketing or communication department.

How it works

  • We will send you a questionnaire so we can carry out a preliminary assessment of your project.
  • We will have a 60-minute consultation by phone or Skype.
  • After the meeting we will send you a report with a summary of the assessment.
  • You will also have access to qualified suppliers from our network of collaborators.
  • If you wish, we will draw up a budget for the project, or a budget from our network of collaborators if the project is not suited for our profile.
  • If you decide to go ahead with the project within the next six months, the cost of the consultancy will be discounted.
